	"# 📊 Telco Churn & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior and its impact on revenue in a telecom business.
The goal is to identify patterns in customer retention and highlight key factors contributing to churn.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand which customers are most likely to churn
* Analyze how churn affects overall revenue
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Support better business decision-making

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Telco customer dataset (includes customer demographics, contract type, tenure, and billing information)
* Key fields used:

  * Customer tenure
  * Monthly charges
  * Contract type
  * Churn status

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show the highest churn rate
* **Long-tenure customers** are less likely to churn
* Customers with **higher monthly charges** tend to churn more
* Certain service types (like fiber optic) have higher churn but also higher revenue

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Churn rate breakdown by customer segment
* Revenue analysis by contract type
* Interactive filters (tenure, services, contract type)
* KPI summary (total customers, churn rate, total revenue)

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Tableau (for dashboard and visualization)

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts
* Target high-value customers at risk of churn
* Improve retention strategies for high-churn services
* Offer incentives for early-stage customers
